BLACK LIVELIHOODS MATTER

Illustration by Sacree Frangine

In these unprescedented times, we as consumers have the opportunity to decide where our money goes.

We have the power to fund and defund businesses we believe help and hinder civil rights.

I hope this website provides clarity to make these micro-decisions easier, and ultimately be used as a tool to help support Black-owned businesses.
